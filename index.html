About Me

In 2014, I began my new career in Tech Support, and then expanded into coding over the successive years. I've taken the U of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp Program and completed classes in Java, Python and discrete math at Metro State University Additionally, I've earned my BA from Iowa State University. My new hobbies include managing Type 1 diabetes in a toddler and navigating the meetings foster parents must attend for the county.
