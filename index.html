About Me

In 2014, I was working in Finance + stock trading floor at a large downtown company. I had a lot of friends at the company, and enjoyed the daily stock market, but I didn't feel like my talents were fully explored. I was spending most of my time exercising Employee Stock Options and decided to leave the company and see what else I could do. I stayed home with my daughter for awhile and then began my job at Code42, to start my new career in Tech Support + Billing, and then expanded into coding over the successive years. I've taken the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp Program and completed classes in Java, Python and discrete math at MetroState University. Additionally, I've earned my BA from Iowa State University in 2006. Go Cyclones!
